About <Me/>

Hey! My name is Ezekial Collins, but everyone just calls me Zeek. Whether I am developing a RESTful API or writing a new song, I love creating things out of nothing. My interest in software and web development began back in 2016 when I became frustrated with the price of VST audio plugins. I searched how to create my own plugins, and ended up going down a deep rabbit hole of development, never to return again!

In May of 2021, I graduated with a Bachelor of Science in Information Science & Technology, as well as a Bachelor of Art in Computer Science, from the University of Wisconsin-Milwaukee.

I'm an open-minded person with grand adaptability, a great communicator, and a creative and critical thinker. A lover of all things music, traveling, and health & fitness.
